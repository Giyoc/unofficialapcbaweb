FREE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

This number comprises of 193 Grade 11 and 437 Grade 12 senior high school students, who studies for FREE in Asia Pacific as mandated by DepEd Order 11 series of 2015 also known as the “Policy Guidelines on The Implementation of The Senior High School (SHS) Voucher Program Under the Government Assistance to Students and Teachers in Private Education (GASTPE) Program

Aside from full scholarship, these senior high school students with voucher receive free one (1) set of school uniform and free one (1) set of school ID, whereas, TVL-IA students have free safety shoes.

    Scholarship in Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teachers Education (BTVTEd)

    53 1st year and 49 2nd year students enjoy their 50% scholarship program with free six (6) units for one semester in Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teachers Education (BTVTEd).

    Also, the school offers free trainings and assessments in Events Management Services NC III, Pipefitting (Metallic) NC II, and Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC II in its Rizal branch, and Agricultural Crops Production NC II in its Bongabon branch for the first semester.

    TESDA Scholarships in Pipefitting (Metallic) NC II and Shielded Metal Arc Welding NC II

    Meanwhile, APCBA opens full scholarship in training and assessment for six (6) batches in Pipefitting (Metallic) NC II and another six (6) batches in Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC II.

    11 of these batches are under TESDA’s Special Training for Employment Program (STEP), while one is under Training for Work Scholarship (TWSP).

    Specifically, 25 students compose each batch with a total of 300 students for the 12 batches.

    Asianites under the STEP will be granted with starter tool kits: 150 welding machines for 150 pipefitting students, and 125 angle grinders for 125 SMAW students.

    Apart from the free training and assessment, each student-scholar in Pipefitting (Metallic) NC II receives an allowance of ₱4, 960.00, while student-scholars in Shielded Metal Arch Welding NC II receives ₱5, 920. 00 from TESDA.

    TESDA Trainers Methodology Level I

    Entering its new academic phase, APCBA now offers TESDA Trainers Methodology Level I to aspiring Technical-Vocation Education and Training (TVET) trainers.

    “It consists of competencies a TVET trainer performing functions of trainer and assessor must achieve. A TVET trainer is a person who enables a learner or a group of learners to develop competencies to performing a particular trade or technical work.” – TESDA

    Other than being a trainer/facilitator, assessor, and expert of TESDA, one can use his Trainers Methodology Level I certification in teaching private and public schools related to the descriptions of the TM’s program.
